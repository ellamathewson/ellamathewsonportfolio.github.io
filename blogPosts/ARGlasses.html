First exercise for my class Current Topics in Interactive Development. Based off of Luke Wroblewski’s augmented reality speculations, I came up with 5 more scenarios of useful AR situations with maximum information and minimum obstruction. The tech assumptions are the same as Wroblewski’s: an AR headset with gaze / eye tracking and audio input control. For these scenarios I took inspiration from my own life and what would benefit me.
