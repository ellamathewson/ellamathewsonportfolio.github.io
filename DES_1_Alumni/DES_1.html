Work About

This poster was designed for RIT's Women in Computing. Working with a few other members on the Public Relations committee, we designed a series of informational posters about RIT Alumni who were also in the club.


It includes information about who they are, what they've been up to since graduating, and where they currently are working. The reason behind the posters design was to promote the club as well as show women in technology different paths previous graduates have taken. You can find more here.
